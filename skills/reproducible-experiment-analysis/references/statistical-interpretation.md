# Statistical Interpretation

- Treat one run as a smoke test, not evidence.
- Report sample count, mean or median, variance/noise, and confidence interval.
- Prefer paired designs when the same input cases can be evaluated by baseline and candidate.
- A claim of improvement needs the confidence interval to clear the minimum useful effect.
- A claim of no regression needs the interval not to show a meaningful negative effect.
- High coefficient of variation means the harness or environment may dominate the signal.
- Outlier removal must be predeclared in the manifest.
